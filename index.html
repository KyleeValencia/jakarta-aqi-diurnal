    About this map

    A research simulation of Jakarta air quality across the full diurnal cycle, simulated on a hexagonal H3 grid (resolution r7, ~1.4 km cells) covering mainland DKI Jakarta. Choose a historical date, press Show date, then pick a location. The page resolves that location to its hex cell and shows the modeled AQI pattern for that historical day, including the hour with the highest simulated AQI.
